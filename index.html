Floriography, also known as the Language of Flowers, is the use of communication via flowers and/or their arrangements by utilising their various meanings and symbolism. This website aims to help keep the tradition and knowledge of floriography alive by providing flower meanings in an easy to navigate and aestethically pleasing manner.

Every month has a flower associated with it, which is reffered to as a Birth Month Flower. The flower that represents those born in November is the Chrysanthemum. This flower represents compassion, friendship and joy, however, it's meaning can change depending upon the colour of the flower. Red symbolises love, white symbolises innocence and yellow denotes unrequited love.
